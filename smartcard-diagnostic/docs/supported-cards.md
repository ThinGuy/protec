# Supported Smart Cards and Readers

## Card Types

### Government Cards
- **PIV Cards**: U.S. Government Personal Identity Verification cards
- **PIV-I Cards**: PIV-Interoperable cards for federal contractors
- **Common Access Cards**: U.S. Department of Defense (testing reference only)

### Enterprise Cards
- Commercial PIV-compatible cards
- Corporate PKI smart cards following GSC-IS specifications

## Certified Readers

Readers that have been tested and validated with this application.

### USB Contact Readers
- Identiv SCR3310v2.0
- Omnikey 3121
- ACR39U-H1
- Gemalto IDBridge CT30
- HID Omnikey 5321

### USB Contactless Readers
- HID Omnikey 5427
- Identiv uTrust 3700 F

### Testing Devices
- YubiKey 5 Series (PIV mode)
- Feitian ePass2003
- SafeNet eToken 5110

## Compliance

All readers listed are compliant with:
- NIST SP 800-73-4 (PIV)
- ISO/IEC 7816
- PC/SC standards

## Validation Resources

For government deployments, validate readers against:
- GSA Approved Products List (APL)
- NIST FIPS 201 Evaluation Program

For testing purposes only, additional validation sources include government-specific approved products lists.
