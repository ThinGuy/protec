# Supported Smart Cards and Readers

## Card Types

### Government Cards
- PIV Cards: U.S. Government Personal Identity Verification
- PIV-I Cards: PIV-Interoperable for federal contractors
- Common Access Cards: DoD (testing reference)

### Enterprise Cards
- Commercial PIV-compatible cards
- Corporate PKI smart cards (GSC-IS compliant)

## Certified Readers

### USB Contact Readers
- Identiv SCR3310v2.0
- Omnikey 3121
- ACR39U-H1
- Gemalto IDBridge CT30
- HID Omnikey 5321

### Testing Devices
- YubiKey 5 Series (PIV mode)
- Feitian ePass2003
- SafeNet eToken 5110

## Compliance
- NIST SP 800-73-4 (PIV)
- ISO/IEC 7816
- PC/SC standards

Validate against GSA APL for government use.
